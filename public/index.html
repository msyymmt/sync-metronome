Connecting without WiFi

No WiFi at the studio? Use phone tethering to create a local network:

  1. One member turns on mobile hotspot
  2. Everyone else connects to that hotspot
  3. Open Sync Metronome — you're synced!

Works with any mobile data (4G/5G). The host phone can also be the hotspot.
