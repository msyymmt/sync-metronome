Privacy Policy

Sync Metronome

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Data Collection

Sync Metronome does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Network Communication

The app connects to a server solely to synchronize metronome playback between devices in the same session. The only data transmitted is:

  • BPM and time signature settings
  • Timing synchronization signals
  • Temporary room identifiers (automatically deleted when the session ends)

No data is stored on the server after a session ends.

Third-Party Services

Sync Metronome does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue at github.com/msyymmt/sync-metronome.
